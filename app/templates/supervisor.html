How the Supervisor Works

Pre-flight check: Every message the auto-pilot wants to send gets reviewed first. The supervisor checks against known rules (too fast, repetitive, banned keywords) and can optionally use its own LLM to analyze the message.

Post-mortem: If a ban or kick is detected, the supervisor captures the last 10 messages and analyzes why it happened. It updates the rules database so the same pattern won't trigger again.

Pattern learning: Over time, the supervisor builds a profile of what gets banned and what doesn't — message frequency, time of day, keyword patterns, response style. It adjusts cooldowns and behavior automatically.

Manual override: Rules can be toggled on/off or adjusted from this page. The supervisor learns from every incident.
